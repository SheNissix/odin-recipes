Tacos Recipe

Description:

Tacos are a traditional Mexican dish consisting of small hand-sized corn or wheat tortillas topped with a variety of fillings such as meat, beans, cheese, and vegetables. They are often garnished with salsa, guacamole, or sour cream.

Ingredients:

  • 8 small corn or flour tortillas
  • 1 lb cooked and seasoned meat (chicken, beef, pork, or tofu)
  • 1 cup shredded lettuce
  • 1/2 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup chopped onions
  • 1/4 cup chopped cilantro
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheese
  • Salsa, guacamole, or sour cream for topping

Instructions:

  1. Warm the tortillas in a skillet or microwave until pliable.
  2. Place a portion of the cooked meat in the center of each tortilla.
  3. Add shredded lettuce, diced tomatoes, chopped onions, and cilantro on top of the meat.
  4. Sprinkle shredded cheese over the toppings.
  5. Add salsa, guacamole, or sour cream as desired.
  6. Fold the tortillas in half to form tacos.
  7. Serve immediately and enjoy!
