Quesadilla Recipe

Description:

A quesadilla is a traditional Mexican dish made with a flour tortilla filled with cheese and other ingredients, then grilled or pan-fried until the tortilla is crispy and the cheese is melted. It's a popular comfort food that can be served as a main dish or appetizer.

Ingredients:

  • 8 small flour tortillas
  • 1 cup shredded cheese (cheddar or Monterey Jack)
  • 1/2 cup cooked chicken, beef, or tofu (optional)
  • 1/4 cup diced onions
  • 1/4 cup diced bell peppers
  • Salsa or guacamole for serving

Instructions:

  1. Heat a skillet over medium heat.
  2. Place one tortilla in the skillet and sprinkle half of the cheese on top.
  3. Add any desired fillings (chicken, beef, tofu, onions, bell peppers) on top of the cheese.
  4. Sprinkle the remaining cheese on top of the fillings.
  5. Cover with another tortilla and cook until golden brown and crispy on both sides.
  6. Cut into wedges and serve with salsa or guacamole.
